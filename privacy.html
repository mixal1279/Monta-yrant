MontaŻyrant · Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MontaŻyrant ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our service to place clip editing orders or redeem a discount code.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google to verify your YouTube subscription, we temporarily receive:

— Your YouTube subscription list (read-only), used solely to check whether you are subscribed to the Edzzi_Minecraft channel.
— Your Google account email address, used only to associate the verification result with your Discord identity during the session.

We do not collect your name, profile photo, watch history, or any other Google or YouTube account data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to verify your YouTube channel subscription and, if eligible, generate a one-time discount code. It is not used for marketing, profiling, analytics, or any other purpose.

3. Data Retention

We do not store your YouTube data or Google account information after your verification session ends. Once the subscription check is complete, all data — including OAuth tokens — is discarded immediately and permanently.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is never passed to external services beyond the Google OAuth flow itself.

5. YouTube API Services

MontaŻyrant uses the YouTube Data API v3 provided by Google LLC. We request only the minimum necessary OAuth scope: youtube.readonly. This scope allows us to read your YouTube subscription list and nothing else.

By using the YouTube subscription verification feature, you also agree to YouTube's Terms of Service.

Google's privacy practices are described in the Google Privacy Policy.

You can revoke MontaŻyrant's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions and removing MontaŻyrant from the list of connected apps.

6. Security

Since we do not persist any personal data, there is no stored data at risk. All communication uses HTTPS encryption. OAuth tokens are used for a single verification request and discarded immediately after.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date at the top.
